TPolls Mini-App - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

TPolls is a decentralized application. We collect minimal information:

  • Wallet addresses for blockchain interactions
  • Poll data you create (stored on blockchain)
  • Voting records (stored on blockchain)

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Facilitate poll creation and voting
  • Display poll results and statistics
  • Ensure proper functioning of the application

3. Data Storage

Most data is stored on the TON blockchain, which is publicly accessible. We do not store personal information on centralized servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • TON Connect for wallet connections
  • Telegram Mini App platform
  • TON blockchain network

5. Data Security

Blockchain data is secured by cryptographic protocols. Users are responsible for securing their private keys.

6. Your Rights

As a decentralized application, most data is immutable on the blockchain. You can disconnect your wallet at any time.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through our official channels.
